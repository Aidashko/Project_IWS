About us

It all started in 1981 on the 6th arrondissement shopping street, Rue Saint Placid, where we opened a boutique of handmade designer shoes.

We quickly realize that these prestigious brands and the prices associated with them are actually meant for a very narrow clientele that does not match the majority of Parisians.

A year later, we decide to launch a project in our image, a shoe brand with our creations, our prices, our vision of style. This new Rudy's seems to be more in line with the market, as we have unwittingly opened about ten stores on the largest shopping streets in the capital, 5, then 8, and today 12.
